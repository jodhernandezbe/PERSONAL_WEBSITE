# About Me

## Who I Am

I'm an Associate Energy Systems Analyst at Argonne National Laboratory, where I'm at the forefront of environmental sustainability, making substantial contributions to the next generation of the GREET model. I specialize in complex software development and modeling for life cycle assessment (LCA), utilizing my expertise in mathematics, sustainability, chemical process engineering, and software development.

My work bridges process systems engineering with machine learning to address chemical risk assessment, end-of-life management, and circular economy applications. I have a deep commitment to fostering efficient, agile development environments and promoting a culture of continuous improvement.
